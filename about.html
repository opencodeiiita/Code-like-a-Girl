__Know us more !__



Every year, Opencode houses a 'Girls only Repository' with the sole aim to encourage female developers to contribute to opensource. This year, Code like a Girl is not only an initiative to promote said students but also build a website that can aid future developers navigate through opensource.
We aim to motivate women towards technology and opensource.
